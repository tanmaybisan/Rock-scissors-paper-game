Game Rules

  • Rock beats scissors, scissors beat paper,
    and paper beats rock.

  • Agree ahead of time whether you’ll count
    off “rock, paper, scissors, shoot”
    or just “rock, paper, scissors.”

  • Use rock, paper, scissors to settle
    minor decisions or simply play to pass the time.

  • If both players lay down the same hand,
    each player lays down another hand.
